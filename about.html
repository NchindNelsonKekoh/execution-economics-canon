About VitroScript

VitroScript is a strategic research and institutional advisory practice focused on execution systems, institutional design, and development effectiveness in emerging economies.

It serves as the institutional home of the Execution Economics Canon, a working research programme studying why policies, capital, and plans fail to translate into measurable outcomes.
